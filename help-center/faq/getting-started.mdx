## Quick Start

1. Go to `https://dashboard.ddddkkkk.com`
2. Click **Sign Up** and create your account
3. After login, select **New Project**
4. Enter your project name and click **Create**
5. Start adding documentation pages to your project

## Need Help?
Check the guide for adding your first project or reach out to support.
