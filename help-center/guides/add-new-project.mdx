---
title: Add a New Project
description: How to create a project in Dddd Kkkk.
---

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Dashboard">
    Open `https://dashboard.ddddkkkk.com` and login with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a New Project">
    Click on **New Project** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill in the Project Name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and Organize Docs">
    Click **Create**, then start adding documentation pages and sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>
