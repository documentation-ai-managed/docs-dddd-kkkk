## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub or Google account for social sign-up

</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">

No credit card or installation required. Dddd Kkkk runs entirely in your browser.

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up takes under a minute. Use the web dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com`.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">

    Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/signup` and enter your email and password.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google" icon="apple">

    Click the Google button to authenticate securely.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GitHub" icon="github">

    Authorize with your GitHub account for seamless integration.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Verify your email to activate the account. You'll receive a confirmation link.

## Set Up Your First Documentation Space

Follow these steps to create a space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Spaces" icon="folder">

    From the dashboard sidebar, click `New Space`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">

    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and select visibility: `Public` or `Private`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Space" icon="check-circle">

    Click `Create`. Your space appears in the sidebar.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Project Document

Spaces contain MDX documents. Create one now.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">

    Inside your space, click `New Document`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">

    Use the editor to add content. Here's a starter template:

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
      ````mdx
      ---
      title: Welcome to My Project
      description: First document overview.
      ---

      ## Overview

      This is your first Dddd Kkkk document.

      <Callout kind="success">Ready to collaborate!</Callout>
      ````

      ````markdown
      # Welcome to My Project

      This is your first document.

      > Ready to collaborate!
      ````
    </CodeGroup>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">

    Click `Publish` to make it live. Share the URL with your team.

  </Step>
</Steps>
